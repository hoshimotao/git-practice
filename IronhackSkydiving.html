    How do we structure the day?

    1. Training

    We teach all the necessary things to jump from the plane without any problems.

    2. Get ready

    We are already prepared, you just need the suit and a parachute. All sizes available.

    3. Fly

    You are ready, and the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

    4. Jump!

    You've completed the hardest part. Just one step left... jump!
